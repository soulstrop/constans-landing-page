# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** August 20, 2025

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

This website is designed to be a privacy-respecting space. Our fundamental policy is simple: **we do not collect or store any of your personal information.**

## Information Handling

This website does not have a user database. When you use certain features, such as a contact form, the information you provide (like your name, email address, and message) is used solely to fulfill that request. This data is transmitted directly to its destination and is **not** stored on our servers.

## Third-Party Services

To provide certain functionalities, we may rely on services from carefully selected third parties. For example, a contact form might use a third-party email service to deliver your message to us.

We choose our partners based on their strong commitment to privacy and security. However, their services are not under our direct control. When your information is passed to these services, it becomes subject to their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to be aware of this and review their policies if you have concerns.

## Cookies & Analytics

This website does not use tracking cookies or invasive analytics. We may use minimal, anonymous server logs or privacy-focused analytics tools for the sole purpose of understanding website traffic and improving our content. These tools do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.
